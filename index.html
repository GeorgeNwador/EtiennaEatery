About Etienna's Eatery

"Where Every Plate Feels Like Home"

A Taste of Tradition

At Etienna's Eatery, we believe food is more than a meal — it's a gathering, a memory, and a celebration. Rooted in the rich culinary traditions of the South, our kitchen serves up generous helpings of soul food that your grandma would be proud of.

From our fall-off-the-bone oxtails to our creamy mac & cheese and buttery cornbread, every dish is made from scratch using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients.

Located in the growing communities of Brookshire and Fulshear, Texas, we're proud to bring authentic Southern flavors right to your table. Whether it's Sunday dinner, a weekday lunch, or catering for your next event — Etienna's Eatery is where flavor, family, and soul come together.
