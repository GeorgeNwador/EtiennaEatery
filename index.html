About Etienna's Eatery

"Where Every Plate Feels Like Home"

A Taste of Tradition

At Etienna's Eatery, food is more than a meal — it's a gathering, a memory, and a celebration. Rooted in the rich culinary traditions of the South, our kitchen serves generous helpings of soul food your grandma would be proud of.

From fall-off-the-bone oxtails to creamy mac & cheese and buttery cornbread, every dish is made from scratch with time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients.

Located in Brookshire & Fulshear, Texas — Etienna's Eatery is where flavor, family, and soul come together.
